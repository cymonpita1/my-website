About Us

Club History

Storm FC was founded in 2026 with the vision of building a modern, community-driven football club focused on youth development and attacking football. Established by a group of players who have the love and passion for football, the club aimed to create opportunities for young talent while competing at a high level. In its inaugural season, Storm FC quickly gained attention for its energetic playing style and strong connection with supporters. The club's identity symbolized by resilience, speed, and intensity was reflected both on and off the pitch.

Match Ground

The club hosts its home matches at St. Janan School Pitch located in Kabalagala, a modest but vibrant venue that quickly became a gathering point for loyal supporters. Training sessions are held at Bukasa Catholic Church Field, where the team developed its identity built on discipline, teamwork, and resilience.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To develop talented players, promote attractive attacking football, and positively impact the community through discipline, teamwork, and opportunity.
Vision: To become a leading football club in Bukasa known for excellence in player development, competitive success, and strong community support.
